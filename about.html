About Us

The Niche Academy is a community-focused school committed to providing a balanced education that combines strong academics, moral character development, and co-curricular opportunities. We serve students from primary through secondary levels and prepare them to succeed in further education and in life. With a focus on islamic values and principles, we aim to nurture well-rounded individuals who are both academically proficient and spiritually grounded.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To empower every student with knowledge, confidence, and ethical values so they can thrive academically and contribute positively to society. Mission

Vision: To be a leading learning community where curiosity, respect, and achievement drive lifelong success.

Our Story

Founded in 2017, The Niche Academy began as a small learning centre and has grown into a vibrant school with a diverse student body. Our development is driven by a commitment to high-quality teaching and meaningful community partnerships.

Programs & Curriculum

We offer a broad and balanced curriculum that emphasizes core literacy and numeracy, sciences, humanities, arts, and technology and islamic studies. Key features include:

  • Structured academic tracks with assessment-aligned learning outcomes
  • Extra support programs for literacy and numeracy
  • Co-curricular activities: sports, clubs, arts, and community service
  • Islamic studies and moral education

Faculty & Leadership

Our teachers are qualified, passionate, and committed to continuous improvement. School leadership focuses on student wellbeing, teacher development, and strong home-school partnerships.

Student Life

Life at The Niche Academy extends beyond the classroom. Students participate in clubs, sports, leadership programs, and community outreach that build skills and confidence.

Facilities

Our campus includes modern classrooms, a library, science and computer labs, sports areas, and multipurpose spaces that support learning and activities.

Accreditation & Partnerships

The Niche Academy is accredited by relevant educational bodies and works with community and industry partners to enrich student learning. And also offering Common Entrance exams and Basic Education Certificate Examination
